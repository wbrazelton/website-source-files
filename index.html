William J. Brazelton

I am a scientist who studies natural microbial communities, specializing in metagenomic approaches. All of my data and code are open-source and publicly available here. I like to collaborate, so please reach out if you have new project ideas.

Current research projects:

Lost City Hydrothermal Field
Studies of unusual archaea and bacteria that live within these unique hydrothermal chimneys in the deep sea.
Atlantis Massif
Explorations of potential microbial habitats within the subseafloor rocks underlying the Lost City hydrothermal field.
Serpentinite Springs
Microbial ecosystems fueled by the products of serpentinization on land.
Bonneville Salt Flats
Robust microbial communities inhabiting the famous salt flats in Utah.
Antibiotic Resistance
Metagenomic approaches to monitor environmental reservoirs of antibiotic resistance.
